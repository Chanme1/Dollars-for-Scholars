The Excelsior Scholarship Campaign: Making College Possible



New York’s tuition-free degree program, the Excelsior Scholarship, is the ﬁrst of its kind in the nation and will help alleviate the crushing burden of student debt while making college possible for hundreds of thousands of students across the state.



As a college education is increasingly necessary to secure the jobs of tomorrow, the Excelsior Scholarship will ensure New York’s students have access to a quality education and the skills they need to succeed.
